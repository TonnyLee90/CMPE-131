This classic Italian lasagna is layered with rich meat sauce and creamy b√©chamel, perfect for a hearty family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan

Instructions

  1. Boil the noodles and set aside.
  2. Cook beef until browned. Add marinara sauce and simmer.
  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat.
  4. Top with parmesan and bake at 375¬∞F for 30 minutes.
  5. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.
